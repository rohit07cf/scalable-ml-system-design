# Templates

Reusable scaffolds for structuring ML system design interviews and study sessions.


## Available Templates

- **Use Case Template (7-Phase)** -- step-by-step walkthrough for end-to-end system design problems
- **LLD Template** -- low-level design deep dive for individual components
- **Diagrams Template** -- guidance on what diagrams to prepare and how to draw them
- **Checklists** -- quick-reference lists for common interview topics, failure modes, metrics, and costs


## How to Use

- Copy the relevant template folder into your working area and fill in each section for your specific use case
- Use the guidance bullets as prompts; replace them with your own concise answers
- Do not treat these as complete answers; they are scaffolding to keep your thinking structured
